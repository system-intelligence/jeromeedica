OEE Charge System

By: Jerome Edica

The OEE Charge System is a smart power control system built around the ESP8266 microcontroller. It allows users to control electrical devices via a custom web-server interface, with manual and scheduled control options.

The system is equipped with 4 relay modules to manage high-voltage devices or appliances, an LCD display for system statuses and messages, scheduled countdowns, and an exhaust fan for cooling.

Building this system involved thorough testing to ensure it could handle high-voltage loads reliably. I experimented with different relay modules to ensure power delivery and various fan configurations to prevent overheating. Finding the right balance between performance and durability was key to creating a stable system.
